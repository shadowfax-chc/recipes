snappy salmon filets

Simple salmon filets with a hint of sweetness.

  • 1⁄4 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 salmon filets

Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, lemon juice. Marinate salmon in mixture for 30 to 40 minutes turning over once. Broil salmon 4 minutes on each side.
