Panko Cod

Cod covered in panko and pan fried.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs Cod
  • 2 cups Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • salt and pepper OR old bay
  • peanut oil

Directions

Heat peanut oil in large skillet.

Season cod. Coat in flour, egg, and then panko. Fry in pan until golden and cooked through, about 1 minute per side.
