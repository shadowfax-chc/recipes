snappy salmon filets

published on April 19, 2016
1⁄4 cup Soy Sauce 1 tbsp brown sugar 2 tbsp lemon juice 2 salmon filets Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, lemon juice. Marinate salmon in mixture for 30 to 40 minutes turning over once. Broil salmon 4 minutes on each side. Source: Kikkoman Bottle
