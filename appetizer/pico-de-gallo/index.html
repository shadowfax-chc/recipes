Pico De-Gallo

A simple, fresh, delicious appetizer or side for any Mexican dish or party.

Ingredients

  • 1 tomato
  • 1⁄2 jalapeño
  • 1 clove garlic
  • a little red onion
  • a bit of cilantro
  • some lime juice
  • salt and pepper

Directions

Dice tomato and red onion. Mince jalapeño, garlic, and chop cilantro. Combine in a bowl. Add lime juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
