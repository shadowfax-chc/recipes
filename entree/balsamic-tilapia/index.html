Balsamic Tilapia

Tangy nutty tilapia with peppers and balsamic.

Ingredients

  • 2 tilapia fillets
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1⁄2 yellow onion
  • 1 red, yellow, or orange pepper
  • 2 tbs balsamic vinegar
  • some crumbled blue cheese
  • salt and pepper

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degres F. Slice onion and pepper into strips. Cook in pan with olive oil. Once vegetables are softened, add balsamic vinegar, season with salt and pepper. In backing pan, coat fillets in olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Place vegetables ontop of tilapia. Add crumbled blue cheese on top of peppers. Bake in oven for 30 - 35 minutes.
