Chicken Quesadilla

Great with pico-de-gallo or guacamole.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, butterflied
  • 2 tbps olive oil
  • 4 tbps red wine vinegar
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1⁄2 jalapeño, seeded and deveined
  • salt and pepper
  • 1⁄2 cup colby jack cheese
  • 1⁄2 cup monterey jack cheese
  • tortillas
  • butter

Directions

Combine olive oil, red wine vinegar, cilantro, garlic, jalapeño, salt, and pepper in food processor until smooth. Cover chicken in marinade and refrigerate for at least 3 hours, or over night. Cook chicken on grill or stove top pan until done. Cut chicken into dice pieces.

Lay out tortillas and distribute cheese and chicken evenly. Fold in half and butter one size. Grill, butter size down and butter other side, flip and grill other side, about 2-3 minutes a side.
