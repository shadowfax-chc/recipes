Simple salmon filets with a hint of sweetness.
<!--more-->

## Ingredients

* 1/4 cup Soy Sauce
* 1 tbsp brown sugar
* 2 tbsp lemon juice
* 2 salmon filets

## Directions

Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, lemon juice. Marinate salmon in mixture for 30
to 40 minutes turning over once. Broil salmon 4 minutes on each side.
